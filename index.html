Our Rooms

Deluxe Room

A spacious room with a king-size bed and ocean view. Includes complimentary breakfast, free Wi-Fi, and access to the pool and gym. Ideal for couples or small families looking for luxury and comfort.

Standard Room

A comfortable room with a queen-size bed and city view. Includes free Wi-Fi and access to the pool and gym. Perfect for solo travelers or couples seeking a cozy stay.

Joint Room

Two separate rooms with a common wall but no connecting door. Great for families with older children who need their own space but still want to stay close.

Connecting Room

Rooms with a connecting door between them, as well as individual doors to the hallway. Ideal for families or groups who want easy access between rooms without stepping out.
